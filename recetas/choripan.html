CHORIPAN

Choripan is a meal of Argentine origin, it is a fairly simple meal to prepare

Its preparation is quite simple and quick to make, adding that it can be eaten at any time.


ingredients:

  • two bread
  • two chorizos

recipes:

  1. Heat the grill with good coals.
  2. Place the chorizos and let them cook for 20 minutes.
  3. Turn them to finish cooking on the other side, for another 20 minutes.
  4. The chorizo is cut in half vertically and grilled so that it is very crispy and cooked.
  5. Cut the bread in half (they can be heated on the grill) and add the chorizo. Serve it with sauces or alone.
