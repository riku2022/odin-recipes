Noodles

Noodles are a pretty normal food, cheap and easy to make

We usually eat them when we have nothing to eat and we have to leave the house quickly, that's when some noodles get us out of trouble


  • noodle package
  • cooking pot
  • water

  1. put water in the pot over low heat(la mitad de la olla con agua)
  2. Put the amount of noodles you want from the package in the pot
  3. let boil for 10 minutes
